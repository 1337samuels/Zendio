CHANGES:

1. REMOVE the sidebar navigation entirely. Remove the Rate Tracker, Smart Timing, and Rate Alerts pages. The app is now a SINGLE PAGE with one purpose: find the cheapest way to move money between countries.

2. RENAME everything from "RemitRadar" to "Remio". Update the logo text, page titles, meta tags, and any references.

3. REDESIGN the main page layout:

TOP SECTION — clean and clear:
- Heading: "Find the cheapest way to send money abroad"
- Subheading in muted text: "We compare routes across multiple platforms so you don't have to"
- Two standard dropdown selectors with country flags inline:
